Musanze Touristic Sites

the key attractions located in and around Musanze District in northern Rwanda. This area is renowned for its rich natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and cultural heritage, making it a popular destination for both local and international tourists. Volcanoes National Park, which is the primary gateway for mountain gorilla trekking, is the region’s highlight, but there are several other fascinating sites worth visiting.

Mount Kalisimbi

Karisimbi is the habitat of gorillas and is known for its exotic plants; it also has four belts of vegetation, from the dense forest at its base to the barren volcanic core..

Ellen Degeneres Campus Dian Fossey

The Ellen DeGeneres Campus of the Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund represents a major leap forward in the efforts to protect mountain gorillas and preserve their natural habitats. It combines cutting-edge conservation science with education and community engagement, ensuring that Dian Fossey’s legacy of gorilla protection continues for generations to come.
